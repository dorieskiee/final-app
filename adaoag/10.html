Delicious Dessert Recipe



Yoghurt Orange
Prepare time
15 mins
Cook time
1 hr
Ready in
1 hour 15 mins

         SERVES: 10

Ingredients

1 medium orange
1 egg
125g melted butter
1Â½ cups (190g) flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 pinch salt
1/2 cup (110g) white sugar
1 small tub (200g) plain unsweetened yoghurt
1/2 cup chocolate chips
a little raw sugar

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 200 degrees C. Grease a 12 cup muffin tin
Peel the orange, removing as much pith as you can, chop the flesh roughly and whiz in food processor until mushy.
Add the egg and melted butter, and process until combined. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar and yoghurt and process together till well mixed.
Finally add the chocolate chips and pulse briefly to mix, without crushing the chocolate chips.
If you're mixing with a spoon, it's fine to add the chocolate chips along with the other dry ingredients.
Spoon the mixture out into muffin tins and sprinkle with raw sugar.
Bake for fifteen minutes or until golden brown. These muffins are delicious hot or cold.
Serve. Share and enjoy!
